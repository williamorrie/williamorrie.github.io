Largest datasets by number of files, then estimate total number of rows¶

698 Datasets held in OpenDataNI, 670 of which are public and have content

  1. House Price lgd level has 99 csvs of ~15 rows = ~1500 rows
  2. House Price electoral ward has 99 csv of ~600 rows = ~60K rows
  3. GP Prescribing has 95 csv of ~450K rows = ~45M rows
  4. 2014 Council Elections 84 css of ~ 50 rows = ~5000 rows
  5. Drainage assets > too messy to estimate
  6. NI Direct Website 50 csv of ~50k rows = 2.5M rows
